Thoughts on Web Accessibility

"Web Accessibility" to me is the extent to which the internet accessible and easy to use for those who are impaired in any way. The internet is an important and arguably neccissary tool today to learn, live, entertain, etc. Due to our relience on the internet for everday uses, Web Accessibility is more important than ever in this digital age. Not properly allowing all types of individuals to have access to the web will only further the "digital divide" and create more enequality both online and in reality for those who suffer from dissability, impairment, etc. Having the proper tools to allow anyone to properly utilize your website is important and neccissary for the future of the web.
