Safety First

While Night City is no doubt an exiting and beautiful place to visit, danger lurks in unsavory areas like any major city. While visiting, espcially for the firsts time, its important to understand how to stay safe. This page condenses information about areas to avoid, gangs that pose threats to public safety, and how to properly interact with the NCPD. By understanding the risks these bodies pose and understanding how to handle them with the information provided, your stay in Night City can be a once-in-a-lifetime experience like no other. Just remember, Safety First!

Pacifica: A Word Of Warning

Warning: The Pacifica Distirct is NOT UNDER THE JURISDICTION OF THE NCPD, and entering the Pacifica District in Night City is not without risks. This area, once a luxurious resort, is now plagued by violence, gangs, and lawlessness. The streets are controlled by hostile factions, and the environment is unpredictable. Civilians are scarce, and even the most experienced mercenaries can find themselves in danger. If you must venture into Pacifica, be prepared for anything, trust no one, and always watch your back. Stay alert and stay alive.
