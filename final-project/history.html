Night City's History

Night City has a storied and intensive history of prowess and victory against the forces of war, the NUSA, extremist attacks and more, this short timeline compiles many of the major historical events that made Night City what is is today.

Night City Founded 1990-1994
Richard Night, with the funds of interested corpos, conceptualized and built Night City as a free economic zone, its once-to-be name "Coranado City" was changed to Night City following the assasination of Richard Night before the city's completion.
The Corpo Wars 2005-2020
The three infamous Corporate or "Corpo" wars defined much of Night City's early years, from land desputes between the mob and corpos, many of the Corpo Wars most important players found their HQ's in Night City. Despite all of the turmoil surrounding each conflict, Night City has always perserviered.
